# AutoStream – Product Knowledge Base

## About AutoStream

AutoStream is a SaaS product that provides automated video editing tools for content creators. It helps YouTubers, Instagram creators, and other video content creators save time by automating repetitive editing tasks.

## Pricing Plans

### Basic Plan
- **Price:** $29/month
- **Videos:** 10 videos per month
- **Resolution:** 720p resolution
- **Features:** Auto-cut, transitions, background music
- **Support:** Email support (business hours only)

### Pro Plan
- **Price:** $79/month
- **Videos:** Unlimited videos per month
- **Resolution:** 4K resolution
- **Features:** Auto-cut, transitions, background music, AI-powered captions, batch processing, custom branding, priority rendering
- **Support:** 24/7 dedicated support

## Features

### Auto-Cut
Automatically detects silence and removes dead air from your videos, saving hours of manual editing.

### Smart Transitions
AI-powered transitions that match the mood and tempo of your content for professional-looking videos.

### Background Music
Access a library of royalty-free music tracks. AutoStream auto-matches music to your video's pace and mood.

### AI Captions (Pro Plan Only)
Automatically generates accurate captions in 50+ languages. Includes customizable caption styles and positioning.

### Batch Processing (Pro Plan Only)
Process multiple videos simultaneously. Upload your content and let AutoStream handle the rest while you focus on creating.

### Custom Branding (Pro Plan Only)
Add your logo, intro/outro, watermark, and custom color schemes automatically to every video.

## Frequently Asked Questions

### Can I switch plans?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

### What platforms are supported?
AutoStream works with content from YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, Facebook, and Twitter/X. You can export in formats optimized for each platform.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial with full Pro plan features. No credit card required.

### What video formats are supported?
AutoStream supports MP4, MOV, AVI, MKV, and WebM. Export is available in MP4 and MOV formats.
